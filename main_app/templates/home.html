About Us

Who are we?



In simple words, we're a bunch of young ambitious people who want to make life easier for others. We're here to help you with your household tasks and solving them together. Our platform links residents and house owners with handy people with different specialities to help make it easier and more accessable for people on both ends.

Our vision



A better collaborative sustainable future together, where people easily come together & make use of technology for a more comfortable life in Palestine.

Our Goals



Our goal is to get you the help you need whenever you need, to make it accessable for you wherever you are with a click of a botton, you'll have the help you need in no time or effort, but at the same time, to provied help for our workers getting more access to the customers and the market, to be able to find jobs easier and faster by being available on the virtual reality. we can only achieve this with all of us working together.
